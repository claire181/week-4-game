Crystal Collector Game

HOW TO PLAY:

  • A random number will be generated at the start of the game.
  • You will use the four crystals below (amethyst, diamond, ruby, and topaz) to increase your number and hopefully match the value of the generated number.
  • Each crystal will have a value between 1-12 but you won't know it until you click it, so choose wisely.
  • You will win once your value equals the generated number.
  • You will lose if your value exceeds the generated number.
  • Tip: The quicker you can figure out the value differential of the gems, the easier it will be to match your score to the generated number.
